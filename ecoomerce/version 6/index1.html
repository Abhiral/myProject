Our Services

Accounting

Accounting comprises of different engines to run an enterprise smoothly. Such as operational day to day incoming and outgoing cash. Visualization of current financial status of company gives the apparent view of overall company.

This modules based on double entry system of accounting which makes the accountant easier. Generating the final accounts such as trading account, trial balance, P/L account and Balance Sheets is just next to a click on a button.

Trading Inventory

Trading inventory module tracks the stock-in and stock-out of an Business Enterprise. Make ease for users to maintain the Stock, Purchase, Return, Sales, Vendor and Client Information in a prompt time. Reports regarding the above mentioned topics can be generated in no time.

Human Resource

Detailed information of employee regarding introduction, job history, academic qualification are the key source to quantify a person to evaluate.

Department wise, post wise and even individually customized salary calculation on the basis of different allowances is the major challenge for the HR every month. This Module makes it really simple with just few clicks.
